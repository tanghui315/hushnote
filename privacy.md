# Privacy Policy for HushNote

Last updated: April 2026

HushNote is a private, local-first recording app for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains how HushNote handles information when you use the app.

## 1. Overview

HushNote is designed to process recordings, transcripts, and summaries on device by default.

We do not require you to create an account to use HushNote.
We do not operate our own backend service for storing your recordings, transcripts, or summaries.
We do not sell your personal information.

## 2. Information Processed on Your Device

When you use HushNote, the app may process and store the following information locally on your device:

- audio recordings
- live transcripts
- generated summaries
- marked moments and session metadata
- app settings and preferences
- optional API key you choose to store for a third-party cloud summary provider

By default, this information stays on your device unless you choose to export it or enable an optional third-party cloud summary provider.

## 3. Microphone Permission

HushNote requests access to your microphone so you can record audio and generate transcripts.

If you do not grant microphone permission, recording and transcription features will not work.

## 4. Optional Cloud Summary

HushNote provides an optional cloud summary feature that you may enable manually.

If you turn on this feature:
- transcript content and summary prompts may be sent directly to the third-party model provider you configure
- your API key is stored locally on your device
- your API key is not uploaded to our servers
- HushNote does not run its own relay or proxy server for these requests

Your use of any third-party model provider is subject to that provider's own privacy policy, terms, and billing practices. Examples may include OpenAI, OpenRouter, DeepSeek, or another provider you configure.

If you do not enable cloud summary, HushNote is intended to keep summary processing on device by default.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not collect or receive your recordings, transcripts, summaries, or API keys on our own servers through normal app use.

Information may be shared only in the following cases:
- if you explicitly export or share content
- if you explicitly enable and use a third-party cloud summary provider
- if you contact us directly by email or another support channel

## 6. Support and Contact Information

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you choose to provide, such as:
- your email address
- device details
- app version
- the contents of your support message

We use this information only to respond to your request and provide support.

## 7. Data Retention

Local recordings, transcripts, summaries, and related data remain on your device until you delete them or remove the app.

If you choose to contact support, we may retain your message and contact information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond and keep support records.

If you use a third-party cloud summary provider, retention of any submitted data is governed by that provider's policies.

## 8. Third-Party Services

HushNote may link to or interoperate with third-party services that you choose to use, including:
- third-party AI model providers configured by you
- external links such as Discord or support email

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

## 9. Children's Privacy

HushNote is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own servers.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page.
